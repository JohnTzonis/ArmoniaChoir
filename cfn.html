International Choral Festival of Preveza becomes a member of Choral Festival Network (CFN)

Preveza International Choral Festival, Greece, has been recently invited to become a member of CFN, the prestigious Choral Festival Network. CFN – the international network for choral competitions and festivals - is a network of qualified international festivals and competitions whose intention is to give fair and transparent participants' conditions to choirs and individuals. To receive such a public acknowledgement is deeply satisfying and is testament to the work of the Festival Committee over many years, and its breadth of vision in developing choral life and experience both nationally and internationally. Preveza International Choral Festival is organised under the auspices of the Choral Society “Armonia” and offers opportunities for both competitive and non-competitive participation for choirs of high international standing.

As a member of CFN, Preveza is now publicly recognised at international level as the pre-eminent choral festival in Greece with a deserved international reputation for its standards, organisation, and welcome. To be numbered as a member of a select network which includes such high profile international choral festivals as Béla Bartók Choir Competition, (Debrecen) Hungary; Cork International Choral Festival , Ireland; European Festival of Youth Choirs, (Basel) Switzerland; Florilége Vocal de Tours, France; International Chamber Choir Competition, (Markrtoberdorf), Germany; International Choral Festival (Missoula) USA; Karuizawa International Choral Festival, Japan; San Juan Canta International Choir Festival and Competition, Argentina; Tampere Vocal Music Festival, Finland; Tallinn International Choral Festival, Estonia; Tolosa Choral Contest, Spain; World Youth and Children’s Choir Festival, Hong Kong; is indeed a major honour for the Festival.

Preveza International Choral Festival thanks CFN – the international network for choral competitions – for its invitation, looks forward to continuing its work in the development of choral life worldwide, and to being a valued member of the Choral Festival Network in future years.
