41st International Choral Festival of Preveza

5-9 July 2023

Download the fully-detailed schedule


WEDNESDAY July 5th, 2023

Church of St. Konstantinos & St. Eleni
  1. 19:30 Acoustic rehearsals
  2. 21:30 Pre-Festival Concert


THURSDAY July 6th, 2023

  1. 19:00 Street performances in varous places of our city
  2. 19:30 Meeting of the choir's leaders & conductors, the official guests, and the Press at the building of Choral Society "Armonia"
  3. 20:30 Meeting of all the choirs at Androutsos Square
  4. 20:45 Parade of all the choirs to the open Guardian Theatre of Municipality of Preveza "Giannis Ritsos"
  5. 21:15 Arrival of choirs at the area of the event, claiming seating arrangements
  6. 21:30 Beginning of the Opening Ceremony
    1. Greetings by local authorities
    2. Presentation of the choirs participating in the 41st I. C. F. of Preveza
    3. The participating choirs perform a piece


FRIDAY July 7th, 2023

Cultural Center of Metropolis
  1. 08:45 Acoustic rehearsals of the choirs – Category F
  2. 10:00 27th International Choral Competition – Category F
  3. 11:40 Acoustic rehearsals of the choirs – Categories A & D
  4. 12:40 27th International Choral Competition – Categories A and D
  5. 16:45 Acoustic rehearsals of the choirs – Category G
  6. 18:00 27th International Choral Competition – Category G
  7. 19:30 Acoustic rehearsals at Courts Square
  8. 21:00 Street performances in various places of our city
  9. 21:30 Open Air Concert at Courts Square


SATURDAY July 8th, 2023

Cultural Center of Metropolis
  1. 09:30 Acoustic rehearsals of the choirs – Category F
  2. 10:00 27th International Choral Competition – Category F
  3. 11:45 Acoustic rehearsals of the choirs – Category A
  4. 13:00 27th International Choral Competition – Category A
  5. 16:45 Acoustic rehearsals of the choirs – Category C
  6. 19:30 Acoustic rehearsals
  7. 21:00 Street performances in various places of our city
  8. 21:30 Open Air Concert


SUNDAY July 9th, 2023

  1. 11:00 Meeting of the board of directors of choirs participating in the Competition with the Jury.
  2. 20:45 Arrival of the Choirs at the area of the event
  3. 21:00 Arrival of the audience & the guests
  4. 21:30 Beginning of the event
    1. Results announcement
    2. The best choirs of each category interpret a Song
