The International Choral Festival of Preveza invites conductors to apply to the 4th Choral Conducting Seminar and Masterclass, which will be realized from 4th to 6th July 2021 in Preveza, Greece.

This year's 39th International Choir Festival Preveza brings two novelties in addition to the concerts of the choir competition and the seminar for choir conductors. The competition for composers has recently ended, and this year the organizer is offering an exceptional opportunity also for young conductors with an International Choral Conducting Competition of Preveza. During the 4th-6th July 2021, participants of the Choral Conducting Masterclass will be able to follow the competition and gain new, important experience for working with the choir. Through discussions with jury members they will be able to upgrade their knowledge and broaden their horizons. Another novelty is the division of the Seminar into two groups. Those who want to upgrade their experience and learn about new literature and those who would like to gain the basics of conducting knowledge and test themselves with easier literature.

The Conducting Seminar is suitable for early career conductors, conducting students with University/Conservatory training in music.

The Conducting Masterclass is suitable for advanced conducting students with University/Conservatory training in music and experienced conductors seeking to work with a high-level ensemble.
